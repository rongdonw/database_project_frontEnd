Welcome!


Dear Graduate Hall Directors,

The Residence Expense Manager is your ultimate tool to managing expenses incurred by your
staff throughout the year. The "Manage" tab above will bring you to pages where you can
add and search through residence hall, staff, program, budget and expense form data.
With all your information in one place, in an easy to use program, you'll have more time for
what really matters -- building community at Columbia!
Most of your residence hall, staff and budget data have already been included for you.
Remember to come back to add new programs as your staff approach you with great ideas,
and remember to add an expense form for each one you hand out. And when you get the
used expense form back, simply enter the details in the "Update Expense Form" tab of the
Expense page, and all the budgets will update automatically for you! Yay!

Have a great year!
R.E.M. Team
